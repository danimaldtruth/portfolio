Nicholas S. Solsman 1984 - 2010

Dear Nick,
It’s been six years since your death and I miss you every day. I will never forgot the lessons you taught me, the music you shared with me, and the films we watched together. I am reminded of you when I listen to this Van Morrison song “Sweet Thing” and these lyrics touch me deeply:


"And you shall take me strongly
In your arms again
And I will not remember
That I even felt the pain."

Thinking of you always.


Love,
Danny
